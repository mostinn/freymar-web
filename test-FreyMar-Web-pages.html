FreyMar's regeneration cycle for aquaculture

Table: Freymar's regeneration cycle
Offshore or onshore fish farm
The fish farm can be any farm where harvest waste is generated
From the farm to processing
Products are harvested and shipped to market. The waste goes back into the FreyMar Regeneration Cycle
Harvest waste processed for media formulation
With R&D, Freymar has designed processing protocols for harvest waste. These have been designed to get optimum microbial biomass and PUFA content in bioprocessing
Bioprocessing
Processed harvest waste is placed into a bioreactor and inoculated with microbes
Fermentation products processed
Microbial biomass from the bioreactor treated in preparation for incorporation into feed pellets
Fermentation products harvested
Fermentation products harvested from processing
Fermentation products incorporated into fish feed
Microbial biomass can either be formulated directly into fish feed or extracted fatty acids
Fish feeding at the farm
Fish are fed on enriched feed pellets
